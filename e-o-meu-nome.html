É o meu nome — Time Capsule Video

Concept

É o meu nome is an audiovisual project developed from the idea of a time capsule: a video created to be revisited ten years later. Instead of creating a self-portrait, I chose to follow the daily life of a close friend, recording her routine, gestures, spaces and thoughts.

Narrative

The video combines simple moments from her everyday life with an interview recorded at the end of the day. These two layers create an intimate portrait of a specific moment in her present life.

Approach

The project focuses on natural observation, quiet gestures and real spaces. The camera follows her daily rhythm, creating a sincere and personal visual memory.

Purpose

More than documenting one day, the project aims to preserve an authentic version of a person and her present, so it can be revisited in the future.
