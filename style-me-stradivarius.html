Style Me by Stradivarius

Concept

Style Me by Stradivarius is a digital marketing strategy created for the launch of a new AI styling feature integrated into the brand's website and mobile app. The service helps users find complete outfits according to their personal style, size, budget and everyday occasions.

Problem

Online fashion shopping can become overwhelming when users have to choose individual pieces without knowing how to combine them or if they fit a specific situation.

Solution

Through a short questionnaire, the user defines preferences, sizes, budget and occasion. The platform then suggests complete looks made from pieces available in the Stradivarius catalogue.

Strategy

The project includes brand analysis, target audience, personas, consumer journey, digital channels, campaign actions and KPIs to evaluate the launch of the feature.
